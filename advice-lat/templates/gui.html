Challenge


Road Deterioration Identification and Prediction: The challenge facing many local authorities is perennially underfunded budgets and few funding prospects. The only path forward is to do more with existing budgets, particularly in proactive and preventative road repairs. As with healthcare, prevention is the best treatment. Earlier road repairs prior to larger and more comprehensive repairs are proven to extend the useful life of roads while saving up to 30% of costs (Asphalt Industry Alliance).

Project


ADVICE, will increase the resilience of the road maintenance EU infrastructure, advising to minimize full-road maintenance actions and early detecting hazards for drivers through the combination of Deep Learning algorithms with imagery and sparse telemetry traffic datasets to early trigger road maintenance.

Around 25% of public Investment in EU is dedicated to transport investments (Road Transport report, 2/2019). A short-term consequence of a close monitoring of roads is an increase in workload for contractors and outsourced SMEs destinated to such reparations. At a second level, early maintenance actions are demonstrated to be more efficient than simple reactive reparations. This better performance directly relates to a reduction in greenhouse gases by up to 2% and decreases of driver's fuel consumption by 2% to 5% (benefits of road maintenance, RSTA organization)

As the experiments advanced, we detected that this given dataset was, sometimes, mislabeled. This could negativately affect the final result of the project.This Label Assistant Tool aims to improve the raw dataset through the inclussion or exclussion of the predicted label of our AI system in an improved second version dataset.
